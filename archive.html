DOUBLE VIKING BRAID

Inspired by ancient Viking works and formed using four trilateral wires, this ring has hidden strength: the 22k rose gold is much harder than traditional 22k gold, adding a sense of vitality to historic symbolism. In a limited edition, only five rings will be produced.

ROSE TWIST

One of a kind ring made out of 22 karat rose gold and a high dome rose cut diamond. This ring is sold. Please get in touch for a custom piece.

LEEN HEYNE x LAELIUS DOUBLE SERPENT RING

Inspired by the historic offerings of LAELIUS NY we collaborated and designed this Double serpent ring. A snake ring, a true timeless theme, like many others goldsmith interpreted before. The ring is bent in 18 karats triangular rod creating a very real movement and set with old-style pear diamonds at the endings. Exclusive available at LAELIUS.
